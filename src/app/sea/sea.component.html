Flowering Plants:
Rose Plant

SunLight :At least six hours of direct sunlight each day.

  • Soil :pH level 6 to 6.5.Soil types are Loamy soil,Sandy Soil,Clay Soil,Silt Soil
  • Watering: Require regular watering, especially during dry periods.Water base to encourage deep root growth.
  • Fertilizer:Use a balanced rose fertilizer or a slow-release granular fertilizer
Sun Flower

SunLight :Minimum of 6 to 8 hours of direct sunlight each day.

  • Soil :pH level 6.0 to 7.5.Soil Types are loamy, fertile soil
  • Watering:moderate water needs and require regular watering to keep the soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.
  • Fertilizer:relatively low-maintenance when it comes to fertilizer requirements.
Hydrangea plant

SunLight :Partial shade in the later parts of the day

  • Soil :well drained but moist fertile soil,pH of about 6.0 to 6.2
  • Watering:Water at a rate of 1 inch per week throughout the growing season
  • Fertilizer:Typically hydrangeas thrive when fed an all-purpose, balanced fertilizer like a 10-10-10 N-P-K or 12-4-8 N-P-K
Aboli plant

SunLight :six or more hours of direct sunlight daily.

  • Soil :well drained sandy loam and red soils with pH value of 6 to 8.07-Apr-2018
  • Watering: Require regular watering, especially during dry periods.Water base to encourage deep root growth.
  • Fertilizer:Use a balanced rose fertilizer or a slow-release granular fertilizer
Lotus plant

SunLight :Lotus need 6 hours of direct sunlight to flower,

  • Soil :a soil mixture about 60 percent clay and 40 percent river sand,pH values that range from 5.5 to 9.0
  • Watering:All that is necessary is a no holes container and a few inches of water above the soil
  • Fertilizer:For almost all lotus you will want a fertilizer with a 10-10-10 or 5-10-10 NPK ratio
Hibiscus Plant

SunLight :Give it around six hours of sunshine, and it will grow well

  • Soil :Hibiscus grows best in slightly acidic soil that has a pH between 6.5 and 6.8
  • Watering:Hibiscus are generally thirsty, but it is possible to overwater the plant
  • Fertilizer:Miracle Grow or other fertilizer mixes
