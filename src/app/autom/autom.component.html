DahliaFlower


Height :15 inches to more than 6 feet tall

ph range :6.5 to 7.0

Soil:Loosened and well drained soil

Types:Dahlia pinnata,Dahlia imperialis,Dahlia 'Bishop of Llandaff'

SunLight : Full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Watering :Drip irrigation is ideal, as it directs moisture to the root zone while keeping the foliage dry

StoneCropFlower


Height :27-30 in. tall (68-75 cm) and 24 in

ph range :5.0 to 6.0

Soil: well-drained soil

Types:Hylotelephi..spectabile,Burro's-tail

SunLight : Full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Watering :During the hot summers, plan to water every 7-10 days. Water every 2-3 week in the fall and winter

ChrysanthemumsPlant


Height :About 6 inches tall although they can reach 2 feet in height when flowering.

ph range :6.5 to 7.0

Soil:well-drained sandy loam of good texture and aeration

Types:Chrysanthemum Ã—grandiflorum,Chrysanthemum indicum

SunLight :6 hours of sunlight each day

Watering :Apply water directly to the base of the plants

CyclamenPlant


Height :6 to 16 inches

ph range :Around 5.8

Soil:organically rich, well-draining soil with a slightly acidic soil pH

Types:Cyclamen persicum,Cyclamen purpurascens

SunLight :Keep away from direct sunlight

Watering :once every week, and less frequently in Winter (roughly every ten days)

PansyPlant


Height :15 to 30 cm (6 to 12 inches) tall

ph range : 5.4 to 5.8

Soil:Rich, well-drained soil

Types:The Blotch Pansy,The Clear Pansy,The Mammoth Pansy,The Whiskers Pansy

SunLight :About six hours of sun daily

Watering :Water pansies regularly through the growing season, but allow soil to dry slightly between waterings.
