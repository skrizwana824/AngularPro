PrimroseFlower


Height :6 to 24 inches

ph range :6.5

Soil:Nice, loamy soil.

Types:Asiatic Lilies,Oriental Lilies,Trumpet Lilies,Easter Lilies,Daylilies

SunLight :Part Sun, Shade, Sun

Watering :Most species of primrose require regular watering

DaffodilPlant


Height :41 cm (16 inches)

ph range :6.0 - 6.5

Soil:loose, loamy, fertile, and most of all, well-draining

Types:Jonquil,wild daffodil,Bunch-flowered daffodil

SunLight : Daffodils prefer full sun they will usually tolerate half-day shade,

Watering :Water immediately after planting and keep them moist until the rains come. Continue watering for three weeks

CrocusPlant


Height :No more than two to five inches

ph range :6.0 - 7.0

Soil:Neutral soil

Types:Autumn crocus,crocus vernus,crocus flavus

SunLight : Full sun for optimal growth.

Watering : half-inch to 1 inch (1-3 centimeters) of water per week,

TulipPlant


Height :between 10 and 70 cm (4 and 28 inches) high

ph range :6.0-6.5.

Soil: well-draining, neutral to slightly acidic, fertile, and dry or sandy

Types:Garden tulip,parrot tulip,lady tulip

SunLight :Plants need at least 6 hours of direct sun daily

Watering :Water once per week week for the first month after planting

HelleborePlant


Height :30–45cm (12–18in) tall

ph range :7.0-8.0

Soil: Loamy soil that is moist but drains well

Types:Helleborus niger,helleborus orientalis,stinking hellebore

SunLight :dappled shade – although some will also grow in sun, others in deeper shade

Watering :An inch of water a week from rainfall or watering, from spring to fall
